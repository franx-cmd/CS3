Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Don't see your question here? You may send us an email through our contact page!

Q.

Why create a website about research?

A.

We believe compiling the information needed to conduct research may help fellow students and more, making it much more convenient for them to follow the guides and create an impactful study. Thus, it led up to us working on this website.

Q.

Is this website updated regularly?

A.

Yes, although it may take some time to add further information.

Q.

How is this website beneficial?

A.

We aim to help people conduct research efficiently by compiling the data needed to accomplish one. Hence, we deem it to be beneficial to those who may need the information.

Q.

How do I contact support?

A.

You may contact support through our support page! It has the details needed for one to contact our team.
